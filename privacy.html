1. Who We Are

D8.Digital (formerly Demonstr8d Solutions) is a boutique digital agency offering branding, web design, SEO, and marketing strategy services. We operate with offices in Houston, TX and Manila, PH.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through our website at d8.digital, including our contact form, booking system, and any communications with us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: what you give us directly, and what's collected automatically when you visit our site.

Information you provide directly:

  • Your name, email address, and any project details you share through our contact form
  • Your name, email, and scheduling information when you book a consultation through our booking system
  • Any information you include in emails or messages you send to us

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and referring URL (collected via analytics tools)
  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address (anonymized where applicable)

We do not collect payment information directly — any payments made through our invoicing or proposals are handled by third-party payment processors.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and project requests
  • Confirm and manage consultations you've booked
  • Send you project-related communications if we're working together
  • Understand how our website is being used so we can improve it
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails. If you contact us, we may follow up about your inquiry — that's it.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses a small number of third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Formspree — processes submissions from our contact form. Your name, email, and message are transmitted to Formspree's servers. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Booking system — we use a third-party scheduling tool for consultations. The provider processes your name, email, and selected time slot. You'll be directed to that provider's interface when booking.
  • Google Fonts — our site loads fonts from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Analytics — we may use web analytics tools to understand aggregate traffic patterns. Where used, these are configured to anonymize IP addresses and not build individual profiles.

We choose our service providers carefully and do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

  • Functional cookies — small files that help the site work properly (e.g., remembering your session if applicable)
  • Analytics cookies — if analytics is active, anonymous cookies help us understand aggregate traffic patterns

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using our website.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and booking records for as long as they're reasonably needed to respond to your inquiry or manage our business relationship. We do not keep data longer than necessary.

If you'd like us to delete information we hold about you, see the section below.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of that information
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications (though we send very few)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@d8.digital with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We'll respond within 10 business days.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we hold. Our site is served over HTTPS. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely. We do not store payment data.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you have concerns about sending sensitive information, please reach out to us directly.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we've inadvertently received information from a child, please contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be noted prominently. Continued use of the site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

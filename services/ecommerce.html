Platform Choice

Shopify or
WooCommerce.
We Build Both.

Shopify
Hosted · Managed · Simple

The right call when you want to focus on selling, not managing a platform. Shopify handles hosting, security, and updates. You handle the business.

  • Best for retailers wanting to launch fast
  • Excellent point-of-sale integration
  • Reliable app ecosystem for extensions
  • Scales without infrastructure management
  • Monthly platform cost: $39–$399
WooCommerce
Open Source · Flexible · Self-Hosted

The right call when you need deep customization, own your data completely, or want long-term cost control at scale.

  • Best for complex product catalogs or custom flows
  • No transaction fees with any payment gateway
  • Full ownership of data and platform
  • Integrates with any WordPress site
  • Requires hosting and maintenance management

Not sure which is right for you? We'll walk through your business model in a strategy call and give you an honest recommendation, including when a simpler option is the better choice.

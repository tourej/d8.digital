A template is a set of design decisions made for an imaginary average business. Your navigation structure, your color system, your font choices, your mobile layout, all decided by someone who has never heard of your company, your customers, or your Houston market.

Custom design means every decision is made for you. Your conversion goals, your brand voice, your specific audience. That's why custom sites consistently outperform templates on every metric that matters: conversion rate, page speed, SEO performance, and brand differentiation.
